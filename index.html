Welcome to the PlotMe wiki. Here you'll find useful content that aims to enhance your PlotMe experience.



PlotMe is a free plugin for all Minecraft users to use. MattBDev, the current maintainer, is a college student that works on the plugin in his free time. He is not getting any payment for offering updates. If you would like to show your appreciation and help him with school related expenses, donate using the link below. Any amount helps.
